who we are
MASUD STEEL DESIGN BD LTD

Founded in 2003, Masud Steel Design BD Ltd (MSDBL), being sister concern of MASUD Group serves as a sole purpose for design & manufacture of supreme quality pre-engineered steel building & structures construction and provide solutions to outright steel complex steel buildings without undermining business ethics, alongside following our motto. “Better Than Best” which already briefs you about its unique working policy.

Discover Us

Strength and durability you can trust, the driving force behind modern innovation.

What Is MSDBL?

MSDBL is a steel manufacturing and design engineering company based in Bangladesh.

What we Do?

We offer pre-engineered steel buildings solutions with the benefits of lower costs, faster construction timeframes, and minimal project delays.
